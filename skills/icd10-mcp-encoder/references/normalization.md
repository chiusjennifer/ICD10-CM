# 詞彙正規化對照表

在呼叫 MCP 查碼前先套用本表。先查左側詞，再用右側替代詞重查。

## 冠狀動脈症候群
- coronary artery disease -> atherosclerotic heart disease
- chronic coronary syndrome -> atherosclerotic heart disease
- CAD with angina -> atherosclerotic heart disease with angina pectoris
- multivessel CAD -> atherosclerotic heart disease

## 高血壓相關
- HTN -> hypertension
- hypertensive cardiovascular disease -> hypertensive heart disease

## 血脂異常
- HLD -> hyperlipidemia

## 心律/心電圖發現
- PVC -> ventricular premature depolarization
- VPC -> ventricular premature depolarization
- sinus bradycardia -> bradycardia

## 結構性異常
- myocardial bridge -> malformation of coronary vessels
- LVH -> left ventricular hypertrophy

## 症狀詞
- chest tightness/chest discomfort -> chest pain
- exertional chest pain -> angina pectoris
- hypoglycemia, resolved -> hypoglycemia

## 呼吸道感染
- pnuemonia -> pneumonia
- pnemonia -> pneumonia
- pnuemonia right lower lobe>left lower lobe -> pneumonia, unspecified organism

## 心臟疾病
- chronic heart disease -> heart disease, unspecified

## 自律神經
- autonomic dysfunction -> disorders of autonomic nervous system

## 排除規則
- 不要把處置詞（PCI、stent、angiography）映射為診斷碼。
- 無明確病歷證據時，避免使用妊娠/新生兒專屬碼群。
- 疾病碼與症狀碼同時存在時，優先保留較具體疾病碼。
